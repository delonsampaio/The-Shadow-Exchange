# Resonance-Flavored Complications

## [Category: Resonance Leak]
* **Scanner Ping:** An **[Faction: Apex Aegis]** Anchor Scanner pings within 200ft. 
* **Intercept:** A **[Faction: The Ministry]** shortwave intercept catches the signature; a bounty on the crew is drafted.
* **Owner Awareness:** The item's owner is suddenly aware of the theft and is walking toward the crew.

## [Category: Cover-Up Exposure]
* **The Witness:** A passerby has a 17-second video already uploading.
* **Ministry Liaison:** The liaison assigned to this district is **[Faction: The Mimic]**; the report will be compromised.
* **The Leak:** A child saw it and is telling a parent, who is calling the police.

## [Category: Subway-Adjacent]
* **Access Denied:** A **[Location: The Subway]** entrance stops letting the crew in without explanation.
* **PTOLEMY's Price:** **[NPC: PTOLEMY]** adds an entry to the ledger the crew doesn't remember agreeing to.
* **Broker's Nod:** **[NPC: Crossroads Broker]** is in the back booth, nodding at exactly one PC.

## AI Facilitation Directives
* **Trigger Logic:** When a roll results in a complication, choose one item from a category that matches the current narrative stakes (e.g., use "Subway-Adjacent" if the crew is near an entrance).
* **Escalation:** If a **[Clock: Detection]** is at 3/4, prioritize "Cover-Up Exposure" complications.
