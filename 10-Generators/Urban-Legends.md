# Urban Legends — Job Lead Pool

The Ritual: each Downtime, one crew member reads small-town newspapers, listens to late-night AM radio, scrolls local police blotters. Roll Attune or Study. On a hit: 1–3 leads from this list (or generated fresh).

## How to generate

For each lead, decide:

1. **The mundane explanation the public will accept** — gas leak, structural failure, isolated criminal act, mass hysteria, prank, weather.
2. **The supernatural reality underneath** — what is actually happening.
3. **The tell** — a detail that doesn't fit the cover story, hidden in plain sight.
4. **Urgency** — when does it stop being a job and start being a Decay Clock tick or an Omen symptom?
