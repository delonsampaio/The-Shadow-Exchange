# Urban Legends — Job Lead Pool

## AI Generation Protocol for Leads
1. **The Cover:** Provide the mundane explanation the public accepts (e.g., gas leak, prank).
2. **The Truth:** Define the supernatural reality underneath.
3. **The Tell:** A detail that doesn't fit the cover story (e.g., "the gas leak smelled like old roses").
4. **Urgency:** Define when this lead becomes an **[Clock: Omen]** symptom if ignored.
