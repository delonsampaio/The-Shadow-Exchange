## Recurring per-score / per-harvest clocks

These start fresh each instance. Don't track them here long-term — track them on the score or harvest log itself.

### Detection Clock — 4-seg
Active **every heist**. Ticks when the crew draws attention. Full = Ministry or Apex Aegis arrives. Resets after the score.
*Sessions 1–2: this is the only clock the players see.*

### Biological Spoilage Clock — 3-seg per Biological
Ticks each Downtime phase. **Paused by Cold Storage Locker.** Ticks 2x per scene on Away Jobs.
*Introduce naturally in Sessions 3–4 — their first Biological starts rotting between sessions.*

### Tragedy Clock — 4-seg
Triggered only by **The Monkey's Paw**. **GM ticks secretly.** Players know something is coming. They don't know what.

## Persistent campaign clocks

### The Subway Decay Clock — 6-seg
`[ ◌ ◌ ◌ ◌ ◌ ◌ ]` — 0 / 6
Visible in the Subway on the transit map (red ink). Advances from Mimic activity, mundane exposure events, and Resonance leaks the crew fails to contain.
- **At 4 segments:** merchants begin packing up. Some Twice-Born flee. Apex Aegis intel begins to triangulate.
- **At 6:** cover-up collapses. Campaign endgame.
*Introduce dramatically in Session 5+ — it should land as a story moment, not a rules dump.*

### The Omen Clock — 8-seg *(GM-SECRET)*
`[ ◌ ◌ ◌ ◌ ◌ ◌ ◌ ◌ ]` — 0 / 8
Ticks as the boundary between worlds thins. **Players see symptoms only.** Separate from the Mimic's plan — this is the world itself becoming more porous.

Symptoms by segment:

| Segment | What players notice |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | Unusually high number of stray animals clustering in one neighborhood. Dogs refuse to cross a specific street. No obvious cause. |
| 3–4 | Electrical interference expanding block by block. Clocks in a six-block radius run slow. AM radio static at all hours. |
| 5–6 | Cold spots in summer. Mundane humans in one district report identical, vivid nightmares — one per block, same dream, none of them know each other. |
| 7 | A child draws the same faceless figure across three separate cities on the same day. The figure appears to be watching something just outside the frame. |
| 8 — Full | A major unscheduled supernatural event hits the city. Something ancient wakes up. The crew did not cause this. It has been patient. |
