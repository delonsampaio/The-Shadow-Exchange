# The Shadow Exchange — Music Prompts

All Lyria 3 Pro prompts for this campaign. Generate in Gemini music chat, save to `11-Visuals/_Saved-Music/`.

**Filename convention:** `music-[descriptor].mp3` — e.g., `music-bookshop-loop.mp3`
**Loop tracks** are marked *(loop)* — add `seamless loop, no clear beginning or end, no melodic resolution, continuous ambient texture` to the prompt when generating.
