# THE SHADOW EXCHANGE: Campaign Bible

> **AI CORE AXIOM:** This is the primary source of truth. All improvisation must align with the "Quiet Dread" and "Industrial-Occult" tone defined here. There is no magic; there is only Resonance and Ethereal Mechanics.

---

## 0. The World Logic (Axioms)

### What the Public Believes
* The supernatural is fiction or hoax.
* Strange incidents are explained as gas leaks or structural failures.
* The cover-up is total and maintained for generations.

### The Hidden Reality
* **The Industry:** A secret ecosystem of licensed hunting firms (like **[Faction: Curios & Chronicles]**) answers to **[Faction: The Ministry]** to maintain the cover-up.
* **The Subway:** An underground neutral ground for **[Faction: The Twice-Born]** and vetted hunters, invisible to the "unqualified".
* **The Cost of Exposure:** Any hunter who allows public exposure (video, witnesses) loses their license and faces criminal charges.

---

## 1. Core Mechanics: The Anchor System

Every entity living in the human world maintains a connection to the ethereal plane through an **[Item: Anchor Item]**.

### Anchor Item Properties
| Property | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Seizure** | If taken/destroyed, the disguise collapses in 1d6 hours. |
| **Resonance** | Items emit signatures detectable by Ministry scanners and Apex Null-Tech. |
| **Contraband** | Seized items are Tier 1 Contraband; mandatory destruction orders by the Ministry. |
| **Moral Weight** | Every item is unique and belongs to a person (e.g., a silver thimble, a child's drawing). |

## 2. The Hub: [Location: The Subway]

### The Mystery of Control
Nobody knows who controls the **[Location: The Subway]**. It simply exists and "chooses" who can perceive its entrances.
* **Hunters who qualify:** Those whose reasons go beyond profit alone.
* **Apex Aegis:** Despite resources, they have never found an entrance.

### Key Subway Entities
* **[NPC: PTOLEMY]:** Sentient luggage. Appraises items, never lies, always charges a favor in his ledger.
* **[NPC: The Fence]:** Buys **[Item: Biologicals]** and **[Item: Relics]**. Pays half for Siphoned goods.
* **[NPC: The Crossroads Broker]:** Found in the back booth. Offers miracles at a "Fair Price" (Memory, Relationship, or Future Favor).

## 4. The Industry Landscape (Factions)

### **[Faction: The Ministry]** (Tier V)
* **Agenda:** Maintain the status quo at all costs. Bureaucratic and efficient.
* **Key NPC:** **[NPC: Director Arthur Vance]** (Accountability) or **[NPC: Elias Thorne]** (True Believer).

### **[Faction: Apex Aegis]** (Tier IV)
* **Agenda:** Monopolize hunting. Treat the supernatural as a mining resource.
* **Threat:** **Null-Tech** (Cold Iron rounds, Resonance Siphons, Anchor Scanners).

### **[Faction: The Mimic]** (The Understudied)
* **Agenda:** Broker a war between humans and monsters to fill the power vacuum.
* **The Tell:** Uses the "wrong memories" while wearing a face (The Casting Mask).

## 5. Monster Mythology: The Three-Layer Rule

No creature is a generic target. Every entity has:
1.  **Holy Weakness:** A specific ritual or material that bypasses resistance.
2.  **Mundane Cover:** Their human daily life and relationships (e.g., night security, librarian).
3.  **Tragic Angle:** The reason to feel regret before pulling the trigger (e.g., summoned without consent).

---

## 6. AI Roleplay Guardrails

* **The Tone:** Use sensory, industrial descriptions (ozone, damp concrete, flickering neon).
* **The Supernatural:** Never use words like "magic" or "spells." Use "Resonance," "Ethereal Bleed," or "Fraying".
* **Dialogue:**
    * **Ministry:** Cold, clinical, and dismissive.
    * **Subway Denizens:** Wary, transactional, and tired.
    * **Twice-Born:** Human first, monster second.
* **The Cover-Up:** If the crew acts recklessly in public, the AI must immediately generate a witness or a Ministry scanner ping as a complication.
