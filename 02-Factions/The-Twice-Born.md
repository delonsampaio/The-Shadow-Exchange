# The Twice-Born

**One-line read** — ancient beings living quietly as humans or animals. Each one is a moral decision point, not a unified group. They barely know one another exists.

## Why they're not really a faction

The bible is explicit: the Twice-Born are not a faction. They are individuals. Each one is a decision. They are tracked here only because the **Twice-Born Favor** mechanic crosses individuals — favors are spendable on any of them, generally.

## The Favor System

Earned by protecting a Twice-Born instead of exploiting them. Spend on:

- **Safe Harbor** — A Twice-Born hides the crew. Clears 1 Heat.
- **Rare Item access** — Trades from their personal collection. Often things the black market has never seen.
- **Lore** — Specific information about the Mimic, Apex Aegis, or the Subway's true nature.

Maximum 3 Favor tokens held at once. If the Subway Decay Clock advances while the crew holds 3+, one is lost as that Twice-Born flees.
