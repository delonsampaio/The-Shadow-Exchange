# The Mimic — The Understudied

**One-line read** — a single entity, embedded in plain sight, manipulating the Subway Decay Clock from both ends. Wants to broker both sides of a war it is starting.

## True name

The Understudied. Almost no one knows this. Emmerich the tailor was present when The Casting Mask was first used, and would share the name with someone who had earned his trust.

## Goal

Destroy the Subway's neutrality — not for chaos, but for control. If the cover-up collapses and the supernatural is exposed, the resulting panic creates a power vacuum. The Understudied intends to fill it: positioning itself as the sole broker of peace between humans and monsters. It wants to be indispensable to both sides of a war it is starting.

## Method

Use **The Casting Mask** to infiltrate positions of trust in both the Ministry and the Subway community. Cause exposure events. Intercept the crew's attempts to prevent them. Drive the Decay Clock from both ends simultaneously.

## The Casting Mask *(Relic — Unique)*

Allows any face, voice, mannerism. Each use costs a Memory — a piece of its actual self. The Mimic has used it for so long it no longer knows its original form. It occasionally reaches for memories that belong to faces it has worn, not to itself. Do not let players obtain this easily.

## Its Face

Plant in Session 1 or 2 as a trusted, helpful contact. Candidates the bible suggests:

- A popular Subway merchant.
- A well-regarded hunter at Harlan's.

## Its Tell

Occasionally uses the wrong memories — a name it shouldn't know, a habit that doesn't match the face it's wearing. Attentive players can catch this if they're looking for it. Plant tells early so the reveal pays off.

## Reveal timing

When it costs the most. Bible guidance: mid-game, after the crew has formed real emotional investment. The reveal is not a fight scene — it is a betrayal scene.
