# Rival Firms

The licensed hunting industry beneath Apex Aegis. Mid-size firms (Tier II–III) and small mom-and-pop shops (Tier 0–I). Apex Aegis is acquiring or starving them out one at a time.

## Harrow & Sons *(Tier I, weak)*

Family operation, third generation. Stubborn refusal to sell to Apex Aegis. Currently under serious financial pressure.

- **Relationship to crew:** potential allies. Older methods, more conservative ethics.
- **The patriarch** doesn't trust the crew's black-market work but respects their results.
- **Standing:** 0
- **Hooks:** their financial pressure could become an alliance request, a betrayal, or a story about a firm too principled to survive.

## The Meridian Group *(Tier II, strong)*

Recently sold a 40% stake to Apex Aegis. Most staff don't know yet.

- **Relationship to crew:** unwitting intelligence source. A contact inside can be cultivated.
- **Hook:** once that contact learns who now owns part of their firm, the relationship becomes complicated. The crew may know before they do.
- **Standing:** 0

## Castellan Retrieval *(Tier I, weak)*

Two-person operation: one hunter, one handler. The hunter is a Twice-Born. The handler doesn't know.

- **Relationship to crew:** the crew may figure this out before the handler does. What they do with that information defines them.
- **Standing:** 0
- **Hook:** the Anchor Item this hunter carries is on the table from the moment the crew sees them coming.

## The Unnamed Firm *(Tier 0)*

Disappeared three months ago. Ministry says they retired. Harlan says they didn't.

- **Relationship to crew:** investigation hook.
- **Where it leads:** an Apex Aegis black site.
- **Standing:** —
