# The Ministry

**One-line read** — The bureaucratic, government arm that regulates and cleans up supernatural incidents to maintain the public cover-up.

## Agenda
Maintain the status quo and ensure that the "Shadow Exchange" remains hidden from the general public at any cost. They believe their cold, efficient removal of the supernatural is the only thing standing between order and chaos.

## Methods
They operate through high-level government mandates, memory-erasure protocols, and elite containment teams. Their "Ministry Couriers" deliver bounties in damp manila envelopes and never stay for small talk.

## Key NPCs
* **[NPC: Director Arthur Vance]:** The stern oversight for high-tier operations.
* **[NPC: The Ministry Couriers]:** Faceless agents of delivery and observation.
