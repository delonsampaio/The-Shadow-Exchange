# Apex Aegis

**One-line read** — the Walmart of monster hunting. Recklessly profit-driven, eroding the cover-up they're paid to maintain.

## Agenda

Monopolize the licensed hunting industry. Acquire smaller firms or eliminate them. Manipulate Ministry bounty lists for personal gain. Locate the Subway. Their corner-cutting is quietly eroding the cover-up — they will not stop.

## Methods

- **Bounty Manipulation** — Ministry contacts adjust bounty lists. High-value targets are flagged for Apex Aegis hours before they hit the public list.
- **Cover-Up Negligence** — Witnesses are paid off rather than properly managed. Scenes are cleared fast rather than thoroughly. Small exposure incidents accumulate.
- **The Acquisition Offer** — At some mid-game point, they will approach the crew with a buyout offer. Subtext: decline and become an active enemy.
- **The Cleaner Protocol** — At Heat 3+ with Apex Aegis, Cleaners are assigned to disrupt scores, damage the bookshop, and make the business unsustainable.
- **Subway Search** — They know it exists, can't reach it, have lost every bribed access. If the Decay Clock hits 4, leaked Resonance gives their intel teams enough to begin narrowing the search.

## Active Operations against the Crew

- **The Mystery Shopper Protocol:** Apex Aegis is actively sending undercover agents (posing as desperate civilians or subway denizens) to hire small firms like Curios & Chronicles. They evaluate compliance, discretion, and operational reliability. 
- **The Compliance Audit:** If the crew rolls a Mixed Success on Double Bookkeeping, Vance's team is dispatched. See the Ministry Audit Clock.

## Null-Tech *(canon equipment)*

- **Cold Iron Rounds** — +1 Harm to supernatural targets, prevents shifting for the scene.
- **Siphons** — Drain magical value from loot mid-heist. Siphoned items sell for half at The Fence. A Tinker roll during Downtime may restore; failure makes the damage permanent.
- **Anchor Item Scanners** — Detect Anchor Item Resonance within 200ft. A crew carrying a seized Anchor near an Apex team is flagged immediately.

## Key NPCs

### Key Figure: Director Arthur Vance
**Title:** Director of Service Accountability and Operational Reliability
**Vibe:** A terrifyingly mundane bureaucrat. Speaks in HOA-style corporate buzzwords. 
**Method:** He doesn't view a loose monster as a tragedy; he views it as a "property management issue." He doesn't threaten the crew's lives; he threatens their licenses and their lease. He is much scarier than a guy with a gun.
