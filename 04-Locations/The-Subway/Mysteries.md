# The Subway — Mysteries

This file accumulates hypotheses and scraps. **Nothing in here gets resolved without an explicit canon update.** The campaign treats the Subway as a living mystery, not a feature.

## The central question

Who or what controls the Subway?

The bible offers four candidate truths the GM may quietly choose between as the crew investigates:

1. The Subway is a living entity with its own agenda.
2. It was built by something ancient that is no longer present.
3. It is controlled by someone hiding in plain sight within its community.
4. It is a collective manifestation of every being who has ever needed refuge.

**Do not decide which is true until the players get close.**

## Sub-questions worth tracking

- **What "quality" did the crew demonstrate that earned them access?** Tied to the Architect mystery (`09-Threads/Plot-Threads.md`).
- **Why does access vanish for bribed hunters?** What perceives the bribe?
- **Why has Apex Aegis never found an entrance?** Is this defensive behavior, or absence?
- **What does PTOLEMY's ledger ultimately track?** The favors? Something else?
