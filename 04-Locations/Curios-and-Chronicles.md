# Curios & Chronicles

**One-line read** — A narrow, wood-paneled bookstore that feels like a quiet fort against the industrial-occult noise of the city.

## Sensory Profile
* **Lighting:** Low, amber-hued lamplight; the green flickering glow of the **[Item: Scanner Station]** in the back room.
* **Smell:** Vanilla, old paper, floor wax, and the sharp, metallic tang of the **[Location: Lead-Lined Vault]**.

Hidden behind a rolling bookshelf. Contains the **[Item: Hunter's Journal]** on a reading stand and the **[Item: Post-Op Table]** for stabilizing or harvesting **[Item: Biologicals]**.
